
# Women Empowerment Initiatives in India

## Educational Initiatives
- Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao (Save the girl child, educate the girl child)
- National Program for Education of Girls at Elementary Level
- Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya (KGBV)
- Mahila Samakhya Programme
- National Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education

## Financial Empowerment Programs
- Mahila E-Haat (Online marketplace for women entrepreneurs)
- Stand Up India Scheme (Loans for women entrepreneurs)
- Mahila Shakti Kendra (Empowerment through community participation)
- Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (National Credit Fund for Women)
- MUDRA Scheme (Small loans for micro-enterprises)

## Health and Well-being Initiatives
- Janani Suraksha Yojana (Safe motherhood intervention)
- Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (Maternity benefit program)
- Ayushman Bharat (Health insurance scheme)
- Ujjwala Yojana (Providing LPG connections to women from BPL households)
- Swachh Bharat Mission (Clean India Mission with focus on sanitation for women)

## Skill Development Programs
- Support to Training and Employment Programme for Women (STEP)
- Gender Sensitization and Women Empowerment programs
- Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana
- Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana
- National Skill Development Mission

## Legal Protection and Rights
- Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act
- Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act
- National Commission for Women
- Mahila Police Volunteers
- One Stop Centre Scheme

## Digital Empowerment
- Digital Literacy Mission for Rural Women
- Women Helpline Scheme
- Mahila-E-Haat (Online marketing platform)
- WomenITES (Training in IT and electronics sector)
- Digital Saksharta Abhiyan

## Leadership Development
- Panchayat Mahila Shakti Abhiyan
- Reservation of seats in Panchayati Raj Institutions
- Women's Leadership Programs
- Political Representation Initiatives
- Corporate Board Representation
